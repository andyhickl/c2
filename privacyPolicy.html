Privacy Policy for HHT

Introduction

HHT is a personal health and fitness analytics application that integrates data from multiple sources including Concept2 workout equipment, Whoop devices, Apple Health, and movement disorder tracking. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Workout Data (Concept2)

  • Training session metrics (distance, time, pace, stroke rate)
  • Performance statistics and historical workout data
  • Equipment usage data from Concept2 rowing machines, SkiErgs, and BikeErgs

2. Health and Recovery Data (Whoop)

  • Sleep quality scores and sleep stage data
  • Recovery percentages and physiological metrics
  • Strain scores and workout intensity data
  • Heart rate variability and resting heart rate

3. Movement and Activity Data (Apple Health)

  • Step count and walking distance
  • Walking speed and step length measurements
  • Double support time and gait analysis
  • General activity and movement patterns

4. Movement Disorder Data

  • Tremor severity scores and frequency data
  • Dyskinesia risk assessments and predictions
  • Movement pattern analysis and symptom tracking

5. Technical Data

  • OAuth tokens for third-party service authentication
  • Application usage statistics and error logs
  • Device information and browser data

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Personal Analytics: Calculate Training Stress Score (TSS) and Intensity Factor (IF)
  • Health Monitoring: Track recovery, sleep quality, and movement patterns
  • Data Visualization: Display comprehensive health and training dashboards
  • Trend Analysis: Identify patterns in training load and health metrics

Data Processing

  • All calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • Data aggregation for weekly, monthly, and yearly statistics
  • Integration and correlation of multi-platform health data

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Primary data processing occurs in your browser
  • No permanent server-side storage of personal health data
  • OAuth tokens stored temporarily for session management

Third-Party Data Access

  • Data accessed directly from source platforms (Concept2, Whoop, Apple Health)
  • No intermediate data storage or caching beyond session requirements
  • Real-time data fetching through authorized API connections

Security Measures

  • All data transmission encrypted via HTTPS
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure API access
  • No storage of usernames, passwords, or permanent credentials
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not create user profiles for commercial purposes

Limited Exceptions

We may disclose information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of users
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View all data collected about you through the application dashboard
  • Deletion: Disconnect data sources to stop data collection at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct data through the original source platforms
  • Portability: Export your aggregated data and analytics

Account Management

  • Revoke OAuth permissions through individual platform settings (Concept2, Whoop, Apple)
  • Clear local browser data to remove any cached information
  • Disconnect data sources without affecting your accounts on other platforms

Third-Party Services

Integrated Platforms

  • Concept2: Subject to Concept2's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Whoop: Subject to Whoop's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Apple Health: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and HealthKit Terms

Our Responsibilities

  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services
  • We recommend reviewing privacy policies of all connected services
  • We use only authorized APIs and follow platform-specific data handling requirements

Data Retention

Session-Based Data

  • OAuth tokens expire according to platform-specific timeframes
  • Cached data cleared when browser session ends
  • No long-term storage of personal identifiers

User-Controlled Retention

  • Data remains accessible as long as you maintain authorized connections
  • Disconnecting a service immediately stops data access from that platform
  • Historical data may remain in third-party platforms according to their retention policies

International Data Transfers

  • Data processing occurs in your browser regardless of location
  • Third-party services may process data according to their geographic policies
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Children's Privacy

  • HHT is not intended for users under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parents should supervise and approve any health data sharing for minors
